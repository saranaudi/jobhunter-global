AI Resume Tailoring

HM scoring Skills grouped ATS keywords Certs surfaced

One-Click Autofill
Saves your profile (name, email, phone, location, skills, experience, LinkedIn) and writes it to job application forms automatically. Works on Naukri, LinkedIn Easy Apply, Indeed, Lever, Greenhouse and more.

Interview Prep
After tailoring your resume, get a personalised prep guide: 5 HM interview questions, what hiring managers watch for in your role, hackathons to compete in, aptitude test simulators, and mock interview platforms.
Company-specific Hackathons Stress tests Salary intel

Application Tracker
Every job you apply to is automatically logged with date, status, and listing URL. Update status as you progress through rounds — Applied → Interviewing → Offered. Full history with quick-filter and stats dashboard.
Auto-logged Status tracking Listing links Stats dashboard

Private by Design
All your profile data, API keys, and application history are stored only on your device using Chrome's secure local storage. Nothing is transmitted to any server — the only external call is the Anthropic API when you click "Tailor Resume".
